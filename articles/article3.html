How to Build a Computer: Part 3



Materials Needed:

  • Partially assembled PC from Part 2
  • A GPU compatible with your motherboard
  • A PC case compatible with your parts


Let's get started!

Get a hold of your partially assembled PC from Part 2 as well as your PC case.
Place your PC into the case after removing all accessories. Screw the motherboard into the case by using the screws included with the case.


Good job. You have installed your case.

To insert your GPU, align the notch, push the GPU in, and screw the I/O shield into the case. Plug in the power supply cabling.




How to align your GPU for installation.


Congratulations! You have installed your GPU!

Place the side panel back on the case, and you're done!

Congratulations! You've done it!

You have now successfully assembled your computer.
